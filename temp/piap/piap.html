BRANDING


The DIY ethos is a big part of this project. it also happens to be a good way to save some coin. Artwork and branding assets are somthing I can take care of myself. I will also build a website for my music. I have already written lots of markup and components, so hacking something together is an easy job I can also handle. I wont be spending money on digital advertizing (not becuase its not effective; I just dont like its existense). My real marketing expenses comes down to web hosting and guerilla marketing (zines, posters, streetart stencils). All said and done my budget is $50 per month ($150 / quarter : $600 / year).

FUNDING                                                              

This project is not a money maker, but it wont be a money sink either. Merchandise wont be profitable immediatly, but it's still somthing I want to have. I currently have a pin mold and & custom packaging I've used for self promo. Im also working on clothing designs, stickers, and patches. I will use printful to dropship items that can be made to order, and release smaller batches of items that I have to order at quantity myself.

I will also host my project files and samples on my website for downloading. They will have a "pay what you want model" without any dowload gate nonsense. Im not expecting this to genorate income, but I think it will spread the good word and pay off in karma.
