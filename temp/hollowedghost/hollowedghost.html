HOLLOWED
GHOST


I started the HOLLOWED GHOST - BROKEN HEART artwork by researching sakura flowers & found some inspiring documentaries on growing bonsai trees. Using a sakura bonsai tree as the focal point fit the project brief well, but came with some challenges.

Bonsais are hyper manacured plants. As they grow, their branches are shaped into flowing curves, & their foliage is organized into tight clumps. I looked at many nature libraries & tree genoration tools, but none captured these traits of bonsai trees. Due to these shortcomings, I ended up making my own tooling with Blender's geometry nodes. The result: a procedural & artist friendly way to create authentic feeling bonsai trees. This tool makes creating varitions for other projects easier & more consistent.

After incorporating the rest of the brief & some compositing, here is where the artwork is at. There is still some polishing left, & I can make adjustments if needed. To get a sense of how the artwork will look on different platforms, here it is adapted to 1x1, 16x9, & 9x16 aspect ratios.


I tried to strike a balance between autheticity & matching the Hollowed Ghost brand by using saturated red, pink, & purple colors. This ended up being my favorite palette, but here are some variations I felt could also work.


One tweak I put up for debate was the rotation of the cover art. I showed a few people 3 variations & asked which they preferred. A small majority thought that "straight on" was the most impactful.
