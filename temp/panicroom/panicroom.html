PANICROOM

PANICROOM tells a story of duality & harship with the sounds of nue rave in an ethereal neo-wastland. The project tackles issues of depression, anxiety, & anger; forces that tear the psyche into opposing halfs. One is neutral, desperatly trying to hold on. The other is a twin shadow, pulling everything into darkness.

This logo was created for the ominous modern vision of PANICROOM. Bold Strokes and extreme width contrast make a futuristic & cutting edge impact. Mirrored shearing embodies a neutral psyche with a twin shadow. Most strikingly, extreme distortions are scars from hardship in an ethereal neo-wastland.
