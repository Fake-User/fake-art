PANICROOM tells a story of duality & hardship with the sounds of nue rave in an ethereal neo-wastland. The project tackles issues of depression, anxiety, & anger; forces that tear the psyche into opposing halfs. One half is neutral, desperatly trying to hold on. The other is a twin shadow, pulling everything into darkness.

This logo was created for the ominous modern vision of PANICROOM. Bold strokes & extreme width veriations make a futuristic & cutting edge impact. Mirrored shearing embodies the duality of a neutral psyche & its twin shadow. Most strikingly, extreme distortions envoke hysteria, distress, & panic.
